ABOUT

Hyperspace pathfinder, is a simple routing engine that use the GeoJSON Path Finder to find the shortest path through a network of GeoJSON.

Acording to Wookipedia: A navigation computer, was a device that made the careful calculations necessary to navigate through hyperspace routes

Hyperspace pathfinder assume the Galaxy as a plane and calculate euclidean distances based on X, Y coordinates.

Coruscant, as the capital of the galaxy, has galactic coordinates (0, 0, 0) despite it not being the exact geographical center of the galaxy.

Hyperspace pathfinder assume that a ship with Hypedrive class 1 velocity is: 1 parsec / 72,31 min

Now you have your own Navigation Computer, be ready to jump through the hyperspace.

ABOUT

Hyperspace pathfinder, is a simple routing engine that use the GeoJSON Path Finder to find the shortest path through a network of GeoJSON.

Acording to Wookipedia:

Hyperspace routes were established paths through hyperspace, which linked major worlds in the galaxy. Those routes were regarded as safe, allowing starships to travel without colliding with a celestial body in realspace.

A navigation computer, was a device that made the careful calculations necessary to navigate through hyperspace

Now you have your own Navigation Computer, be ready to jump through the hyperspace.
